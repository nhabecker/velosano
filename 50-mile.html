VeloSano 2018 Training Guides

50 Mile Training Guide

For those looking for a bit of a challenge! Heading east from downtown Cleveland, this ride takes you through Cleveland Clinic’s Main Campus, through beautiful Shaker Heights, the scenic South Chagrin Reservation, and climbing out of the Chagrin River Valley. We finish downtown where it all began, at Mall B with a Finish Line Celebration.
