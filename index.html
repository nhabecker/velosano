What is Velosano?

VeloSano is a major community initiative in Cleveland, Ohio in which participants raise money for cancer research through the sport of cycling. 100% of the dollars raised by VeloSano participants benefits lifesaving cancer research at Cleveland Clinic. In four years, over $12 million has been generated and is already hard at work in the fight against cancer.
