VeloSano 2018 Training Guides

25 Mile Training Guide

A mostly flat ride perfect for the casual rider. Heading east from downtown Cleveland, this ride takes you through Cleveland Clinic’s Main Campus through beautiful Shaker Heights. We have one slight hill for you around mile 5 – but don’t worry, you’ve got this! We finish downtown where it all began, at Mall B with a Finish Line Celebration.
