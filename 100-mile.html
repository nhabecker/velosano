100+ Mile Training Guide

Tackle a challenging century through Cleveland’s East side! Our 100-mile route follows our 75-mile route but offers an additional 25-mile spur around mile 47. Heading east from downtown Cleveland, this ride takes you through Cleveland Clinic’s Main Campus past the Taussig Cancer Center and where VeloSano dollars are hard at work. Ride through beautiful Shaker Heights and the scenic South Chagrin Reservation. You'll then ride east towards Bainbridgeand north near Punderson State Park. The additional 25 mile spur loops north around Kirtland Hills and The Holden Arboretum before returning south through Chesterland, and reconnecting with the 75-mile route for the cruise back west. Climbing out of the Chagrin River Valley we complete the loop and end at Mall B with a Finish Line Celebration.
